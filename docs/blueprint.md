## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A rich, deep dark walnut brown (#51260C) to convey luxury and earthiness, serving as the dominant element in branding and key UI components.
- Background color: A subtle, warm off-white cream (#FDF9F5) that provides a clean, premium canvas for content while harmonizing with the natural wood theme.
- Accent color: A distinguished coppery-gold (#E67750) for 'gold details', highlights, and interactive elements, adding a touch of elegance and visual interest.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end, and fashionable feel in titles and hero sections. Body font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for clean readability and a modern touch in descriptive text.
- Minimalist, organic line icons with a premium aesthetic to complement the natural wood products and luxurious design, used subtly throughout the interface.
- Responsive layout design ensuring a seamless and optimal viewing experience across all devices, with a hero section prominently featuring high-quality wood visuals and product cards utilizing a glassmorphism effect and soft shadows.
- Subtle, modern animation cues and elegant transition effects (fade, scroll) to enhance user interaction and create a dynamic, premium browsing experience without overwhelming the user.
